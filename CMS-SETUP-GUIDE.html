Brightstone CMS — Setup Guide

One-time setup (you do this). After that, client edits everything on their own.

What the client can edit (after setup)

  1. Go to yoursite.com/admin and log in with their email
  2. Click any section: Hero, Services, Quote, Difference, Footer
  3. Change text, update WhatsApp number, edit card descriptions
  4. Click Save and the site updates live in about 30 seconds
  5. No coding. No developers. No Wix subscription.
